Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Hop 'n Bop ("the Game") is published by Snoring Cat ("we", "us", "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in via Google, Facebook, Steam, Epic, Apple, or Game Center, we receive your display name and a provider-specific user ID. We do not receive or store your password.
  • Device identifier: If you play anonymously, we generate a random device ID stored locally on your device.
  • Gameplay data: Match results, scores, kills, deaths, and other in-game statistics.
  • Technical data: Game version, platform, and crash reports (stack traces, no personal data).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account.
  • Track gameplay statistics and display leaderboards.
  • Match you with other players.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve the Game.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States. Authentication providers (Google, Facebook, etc.) are contacted only during sign-in and receive no gameplay data from us.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and gameplay data for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time (see below).

5. Your Rights

  • Access and export: You can request a copy of all data associated with your account.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. See our Data Deletion Policy.
  • Do not sell: We do not sell personal information. No opt-out is necessary.

To exercise these rights, use the in-game account settings or email us at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for players aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit and encrypt sensitive data at rest. Access to player data is restricted to authorized systems.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
